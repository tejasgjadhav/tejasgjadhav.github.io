01 — About

Twelve years.
Four institutions.

Tejas Jadhav, CFA, FRM is a finance professional with twelve years of experience across four global institutions. His career reflects one consistent belief — finance runs better when data, risk, and technology work together.

He has built and led projects across Retail Banking, Market Risk, Climate Risk, and Wealth Management at Citi, HDFC Bank, and leading Swiss Banks — operating on both sides of the desk, managing HNI client portfolios while building the technology platforms that support investment and risk decisions.

He currently works on AI and Finance integration at a large MNC, building institutional-grade AI workflows. He is also a published author on AI applications in finance — his books are used by equity analysts, portfolio managers, and investment bankers across India and globally.

Outside his primary role, he serves as guest faculty at leading management institutes and as an equity research judge at Bloomberg Club competitions, where he mentors the next generation of analysts on both financial rigour and the practical use of AI.
