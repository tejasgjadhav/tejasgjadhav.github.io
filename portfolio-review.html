Your Data is Safe — Here's Exactly How
PDF never leaves your device
Read entirely within your browser using PDF.js. Never uploaded to any server, cloud or database.
Only Mutual Fund section is sent
Extracts only the Mutual Fund Folios (F) section. Your equity holdings · demat account numbers · PAN · Aadhaar · email · mobile are never read or transmitted.

Even within the MF section, your browser automatically strips PAN · Aadhaar · email · mobile · IFSC · demat IDs · PRAN before analysis. Only fund names, NAV, cost and returns reach the engine.
Password stays in your browser
