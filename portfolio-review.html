Your Data is Safe — Here's Exactly How
PDF never leaves your device
Your eCAS PDF is read entirely within your browser using PDF.js — an open source library. The file is never uploaded to any server, cloud or database.
Only Mutual Fund section is sent
The tool extracts only the Mutual Fund Folios (F) section of your eCAS. Your equity holdings · demat account numbers · PAN · Aadhaar · email · mobile are never read or transmitted.
Sensitive data scrubbing — before anything is sent
Even within the MF section, your code automatically strips PAN · Aadhaar · email · mobile · IFSC · demat IDs · PRAN before the data leaves your browser. Only fund names, NAV, cost and returns reach the analysis engine.
Password stays in your browser
Your PAN (used as PDF password) is used only to decrypt the PDF locally in your browser. It is never sent to any server, stored in any database or logged anywhere.
No account, no login, no tracking
This tool requires no sign-up. No cookies are set. No analytics or tracking scripts are loaded. Nothing is remembered between sessions.
Educational use only — Not SEBI registered
This analysis is for educational and informational purposes only. Tejas Jadhav is not a SEBI registered investment advisor. Nothing here constitutes investment advice. Please consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
