## Global production notes
- **Palette:** pick one dark base (navy/graphite) + one accent, and reuse across all 5 images so the section reads as one system.
- **Text-in-image:** keep any text in images minimal and large — Amazon shrinks A+ images hard on mobile. The desk strip in the hero is the only dense text; keep it ≥ 24px equivalent.
- **Do NOT include** in any module: price, "bestseller"/rank claims, time-limited language, contact info/URLs, or competitor product names — all are A+ rejection triggers.
- **Credential usage (CFA Institute / GARP standards):** the CFA and FRM marks appear **only as post-nominals attached to the author's name** — "Tejas Jadhav, CFA, FRM" — and never as nouns ("a CFA"), never as a standalone badge, and never in a way implying the designation endorses the book or promises investment results. Keep it that way in every edit.
- **Approval time:** A+ Content typically reviews in up to ~7 business days after you submit.
