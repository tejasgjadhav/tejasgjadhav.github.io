'''Tejas Jadhav''' (born in Pune, India) is an Indian financial professional, author, and consultant specialising in artificial intelligence applications in finance. He holds the [[Chartered Financial Analyst]] (CFA) designation from the [[CFA Institute]] and the [[Financial Risk Manager]] (FRM) certification from the [[Global Association of Risk Professionals]] (GARP).<ref name="cfa">{{cite web |title=Tejas Jadhav — Official Portfolio |url=https://tejasgjadhav.github.io/ |access-date=2026-06-03}}</ref> He has authored five books on finance and AI, including ''AI Prompts for Financial Analysis'', which reached the bestseller list in the Finance category on [[Amazon India]].<ref name="amazon-author">{{cite web |title=Tejas Jadhav — Amazon Author Page |url=https://www.amazon.com/stores/author/B0GTBWDWML |publisher=Amazon |access-date=2026-06-03}}</ref>

==Early life and education==
Tejas Jadhav was educated in [[Pune]], [[Maharashtra]], India. He completed postgraduate studies at the [[Sydenham Institute of Management Studies, Research and Entrepreneurship Education]] in [[Mumbai]].<ref name="portfolio">{{cite web |title=Tejas Jadhav — Official Portfolio |url=https://tejasgjadhav.github.io/ |access-date=2026-06-03}}</ref> He subsequently completed a programme at [[WorldQuant University]], an online quantitative finance institution.

He earned the [[Financial Risk Manager]] (FRM) certification from [[GARP]] and in 2025 qualified as a [[Chartered Financial Analyst]] (CFA) through the [[CFA Institute]].

==Career==
Jadhav has over twelve years of professional experience across retail banking, market risk, climate risk, and wealth management. He has held positions at [[Citigroup|Citi]], [[HDFC Bank]], and leading Swiss financial institutions.<ref name="mint">{{cite news |title=Tejas Jadhav, CFA, FRM featured in Mint |url=https://pressreader.com/article/281702621142581 |newspaper=[[Mint (newspaper)|Mint]] |access-date=2026-06-03}}</ref>

His areas of expertise include [[Financial risk management|financial risk management]], [[market risk]], [[climate risk]] in financial services, [[wealth management]], [[equity research]], and financial modelling under regulatory frameworks including the [[Fundamental Review of the Trading Book]] (FRTB).

He has applied [[large language model]]s and [[artificial intelligence]] tools to financial workflows, focusing on prompt engineering for financial analysis and AI-assisted investment research. He also operates Topmate consultations for finance professionals on AI and finance topics.
